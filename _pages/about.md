## Research Focus
My research explores the integration of large language models (LLMs) with complex optimization and decision-making problems, with an emphasis on:

- LLM-driven methods for combinatorial optimization  
- Intelligent scheduling in satellite observation systems  
- Multi-value alignment and robustness of LLMs in complex environments
