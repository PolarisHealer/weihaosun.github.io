## About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Management Science and Engineering at the School of Management, Hefei University of Technology. My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and operations research, with a focus on leveraging large language models to solve complex real-world problems.

## Research Interests

My research interests include:

- **LLM-driven Combinatorial Optimization**: Exploring how large language models can enhance decision-making in complex optimization problems
- **Satellite Observation Task Planning**: Developing intelligent scheduling algorithms for satellite observation missions
- **Value Alignment in Large Language Models**: Investigating multi-dimensional value alignment mechanisms for AI systems
